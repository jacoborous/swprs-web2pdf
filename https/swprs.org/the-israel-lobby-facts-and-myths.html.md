# The Israel Lobby: Facts and Myths

<div class="post-content clear">

An overview of high-quality documentaries, studies and reports (ordered
chronologically).

**Note**: These works do not advocate anti-Jewish positions.

1.  [The Lobby — USA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MnzRsfMqOqI): An
    investigative documentary (Al Jazeera, 180 minutes,
    [2018](https://electronicintifada.net/blogs/asa-winstanley/al-jazeera-denies-qatari-emir-censored-israel-lobby-film))
2.  [Israel and Internet
    Censorship](http://www.unz.com/article/how-israel-and-its-partisans-work-to-censor-the-internet/)
    (Alison Weir, The Ron Unz Review, 2018;
    [Video](https://www.bitchute.com/video/GsNHqtjALSHJ/))
3.  [The Lobby in Britain](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ceCOhdgRBoc):
    An investigative documentary (Al Jazeera, 90 minutes, 2017)
4.  [America’s Top 20 Billionaires Who Support
    Israel](https://www.breakingisraelnews.com/52318/exclusive-americas-top-20-richest-pro-israel-zionaires-technology-and-business/)
    (Abra Forman, 2015)
5.  [The Israel Lobby – A Partial
    List](https://councilforthenationalinterest.org/?page_id=33) (The
    Council For The National Interest, 2012)
6.  [»Jews Do Control The
    Media«](https://web.archive.org/web/20120705065748/http://blogs.timesofisrael.com/jews-do-control-the-media/)
    (Elad Nehorai, The Times of Israel, 2012)
7.  [Defamation: The Movie](https://archive.org/details/Defamation)
    (Yoav Shamir, First Run Features, 90 minutes,
    [2009](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Defamation_\(film\)))
8.  [Do Jews run
    Hollywood?](http://articles.latimes.com/2008/dec/19/opinion/oe-stein19)
    (Joel Stein, Los Angeles Times, 2008)
9.  [Do Jews dominate in American
    Media?](https://mondoweiss.net/2008/02/do-jews-dominat/) (Philip
    Weiss, Mondoweiss, 2008)
10. [The Israel Lobby in the
    US](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N294FMDok98) (VPRO Documentary,
    50 minutes, 2007)
11. [The Israel
    Lobby](https://www.lrb.co.uk/v28/n06/john-mearsheimer/the-israel-lobby)
    (Profs. Mearsheimer & Walt, London Review of Books,
    [2006](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Israel_Lobby_and_U.S._Foreign_Policy))
12. [Jewish Power: Inside The American Jewish
    Establishment](https://www.amazon.com/Jewish-Power-Inside-American-Establishment/dp/0201327988)
    (J.J. Goldberg, 1997)
13. [A lecture by Mossad whistleblower Victor
    Ostrovsky](https://archive.org/details/victor-ostrovsky-1995)
    (C-SPAN, 1995)

#### Read also

  - [The American Empire and its
    Media](https://swprs.org/the-american-empire-and-its-media/)
  - [The Logic of US Foreign
    Policy](https://swprs.org/us-foreign-policy/)
  - [The Media Navigator](https://swprs.org/media-navigator/)
